# 🏥 Hospital Information Management System (HIMS)

A full-stack Hospital Information Management System (HIMS) designed to streamline and automate hospital operations. The system provides a centralized platform for managing patients, doctors, appointments, medical records, laboratory services, pharmacy inventory, and billing processes.

---

## 📌 Features

### 👤 Patient Management
- Patient registration and profile management
- Medical history tracking
- Admission and discharge records

### 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Management
- Doctor profiles and specialization details
- Availability and schedule management

### 📅 Appointment Management
- Book, reschedule, and cancel appointments
- Appointment status tracking

### 🧪 Laboratory Management
- Laboratory test requests
- Upload and manage test reports
- View patient test history

### 💊 Pharmacy Management
- Medicine inventory management
- Stock monitoring and alerts
- Prescription handling

### 💰 Billing and Payments
- Invoice generation
- Payment history management
- Billing reports

### 🔐 Authentication & Authorization
- Role-based access control
- Admin Dashboard
- Doctor Dashboard
- Receptionist Dashboard
- Patient Portal
